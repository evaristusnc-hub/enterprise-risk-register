# Risk Governance

This section defines the governance structure used to oversee enterprise risk management activities.

## Governance Components

- Risk Governance Charter
- Risk Ownership Matrix
- Risk Escalation Process
- Executive Risk Reporting

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Board of Directors
Provides oversight of enterprise risk.

### Executive Leadership
Reviews enterprise risks and approves treatment strategies.

### Risk Owners
Manage and mitigate assigned risks.

### Internal Audit
Provides independent assurance over risk management activities.
