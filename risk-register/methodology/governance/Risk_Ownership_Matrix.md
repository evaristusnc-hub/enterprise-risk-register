# Risk Ownership Matrix

| Risk Category | Risk Owner |
|--------------|------------|
| Cybersecurity Risk | CISO |
| Compliance Risk | Compliance Manager |
| Third-Party Risk | Vendor Risk Manager |
| Operational Risk | Operations Director |
| Financial Risk | CFO |
| Strategic Risk | Executive Leadership |

## Ownership Principles

- Every risk must have an assigned owner.
- Risk owners are responsible for mitigation activities.
- Risk owners provide periodic status updates.
- Escalation is required for high and critical risks.
