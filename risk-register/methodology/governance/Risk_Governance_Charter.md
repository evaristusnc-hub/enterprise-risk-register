# Risk Governance Charter

## Purpose

The purpose of this charter is to establish a governance framework for identifying, assessing, managing, monitoring, and reporting enterprise risks.

## Scope

This charter applies to all business units, technology functions, third-party relationships, and operational processes.

## Governance Objectives

- Promote risk-aware decision making
- Define accountability for risk ownership
- Establish risk reporting requirements
- Support regulatory and compliance obligations
- Enable continuous risk monitoring

## Risk Oversight Structure

### Board of Directors
Provides strategic oversight of enterprise risk.

### Executive Leadership Team
Reviews enterprise risk exposure and approves treatment strategies.

### Risk Management Function
Facilitates risk assessments, monitoring, and reporting.

### Risk Owners
Manage risks within their assigned business areas.

### Internal Audit
Provides independent assurance regarding risk management effectiveness.

## Reporting Frequency

- Monthly Risk Reviews
- Quarterly Executive Risk Reports
- Annual Enterprise Risk Assessment

## Approval

Approved by Executive Leadership.
