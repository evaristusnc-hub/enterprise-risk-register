# Risk Assessment Methodology

This document outlines the methodology used to identify, assess, score, prioritize, and monitor enterprise risks.

## Risk Assessment Process

1. Risk Identification
2. Risk Analysis
3. Risk Scoring
4. Risk Prioritization
5. Risk Treatment
6. Residual Risk Evaluation
7. Continuous Monitoring

## Scoring Model

Likelihood: 1–5

Impact: 1–5

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact
