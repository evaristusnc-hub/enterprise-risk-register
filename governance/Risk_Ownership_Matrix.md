# Risk Ownership Matrix

| Risk Category      | Risk Owner                                |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------- |
| Cybersecurity Risk | Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) |
| Compliance Risk    | Compliance Manager                        |
| Third-Party Risk   | Vendor Risk Manager                       |
| Operational Risk   | Operations Director                       |
| Financial Risk     | Chief Financial Officer (CFO)             |
| Strategic Risk     | Executive Leadership Team                 |
| Privacy Risk       | Privacy Officer                           |
| Technology Risk    | IT Director                               |

## Ownership Principles

* Every risk must have an assigned owner.
* Risk owners are accountable for managing and mitigating risks.
* Risk owners must provide periodic status updates.
* High and Critical risks must be escalated to Executive Leadership.
* Risk ownership assignments are reviewed annually.

## Escalation Criteria

### High Risk

* Escalate to Department Leadership
* Monthly monitoring required

### Critical Risk

* Escalate to Executive Leadership
* Immediate remediation planning required
* Executive reporting required until closure

## Review Frequency

* Monthly Risk Review Meetings
* Quarterly Executive Risk Reviews
* Annual Risk Ownership Assessment
